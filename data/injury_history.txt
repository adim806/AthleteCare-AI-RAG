SOURCE: injury_history.txt | DOMAIN: injury_records | UPDATED: November 2024
CHUNK_FORMAT: INJURY_RECORD | Each record is self-contained and independently retrievable.

Classification reference:
  - Grade I = mild (return 5–10 days)
  - Grade II = moderate (return 2–6 weeks)
  - Grade III = severe (return 3–9 months)
  - ACL rupture (return 9–12 months)
  - Stress fracture (return 10–16 weeks)

Policy: Recurring injuries in the same anatomical region within 12 months must be reviewed at the monthly medical meeting. Any re-injury to a previously Grade III or surgically repaired structure must be referred immediately to Dr. Anat Shapira regardless of apparent severity.
