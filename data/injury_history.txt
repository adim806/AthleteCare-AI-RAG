FC Velocity — Injury History Log (2021–2024)

Each injury is recorded as a self-contained summary. Injury classification: Grade I = mild (return 5–10 days), Grade II = moderate (return 2–6 weeks), Grade III = severe (return 3–9 months), ACL rupture (return 9–12 months), stress fracture (return 10–16 weeks).

Recurring injuries in the same anatomical region within 12 months must be reviewed at the monthly medical meeting. Any re-injury to a previously Grade III or surgically repaired structure must be referred immediately to Dr. Anat Shapira regardless of apparent severity.
