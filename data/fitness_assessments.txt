RECORD_TYPE: FITNESS_BENCHMARK
SECTION: Test battery benchmarks

Q: What is the Yo-Yo IR2 benchmark for outfield players?
A: Minimum 1080m. Elite: 1400m+.

Q: What is the 30-metre sprint benchmark for outfield players?
A: Below 4.0 seconds. Elite: below 3.8 seconds.

Q: What is the 5-10-5 agility benchmark for outfield players?
A: Below 4.4 seconds.

Q: What is the single-leg hop test RTP threshold?
A: LSI ≥90%. Calculated as injured ÷ uninjured × 100.

Q: What is the isokinetic H:Q ratio benchmark?
A: 0.60–0.70. Below 0.55 = immediate referral to hamstring strengthening.

Q: What sit-and-reach score triggers a flexibility programme?
A: Below 28cm. Note: Okafor previously below benchmark — resolved April 2026 (now 30cm).

Q: What body fat benchmarks apply?
A: Outfield players: 8–14%. Goalkeepers: 10–16%.

Q: What HRV drop triggers load management review?
A: More than 20% from personal pre-season baseline on consecutive mornings.

RECORD_TYPE: FITNESS_POLICY
SECTION: Load monitoring — ACWR

Q: What GPS system does FC Velocity use?
A: Catapult Sport GPS system.

Q: What GPS metrics are tracked daily?
A: Total distance, high-speed running (above 21 km/h), sprint distance (above 25 km/h), Player Load, ACWR.

Q: How is ACWR calculated?
A: Current week's training load ÷ rolling 4-week average training load.

Q: What is the safe ACWR zone?
A: 0.80–1.30.

Q: What ACWR indicates high injury risk?
A: Above 1.50. Tal Ben-David immediately modifies next session load and notifies responsible physiotherapist. Example: Levi and Alvarez reached >1.5 in February 2026 — managed successfully.
