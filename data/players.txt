MEDICAL BASELINE NORMS — FC VELOCITY
RECORD_TYPE: SQUAD_NORMS

Q: What is the resting heart rate target for FC Velocity players?
A: 42–58 bpm. Values above 60 bpm after a rest day may indicate insufficient recovery or illness.

Q: What is the normal blood pressure range?
A: 100/60 – 130/80 mmHg.

Q: What is the body fat benchmark for outfield players and goalkeepers?
A: Outfield players: 8–14%. Goalkeepers: 10–16%.

Q: What is the minimum VO2 max for outfield players and goalkeepers?
A: Outfield players: 58 ml/kg/min minimum. Goalkeepers: 52 ml/kg/min minimum.

Q: What is the minimum sit-and-reach flexibility score?
A: 28 cm. Players below this threshold are enrolled in a daily posterior chain flexibility programme.

Q: What is the minimum maximum sprint speed for outfield players?
A: 30 km/h.

Q: What is the target hamstring-to-quadriceps strength ratio (H:Q)?
A: 0.60–0.70 assessed by isokinetic dynamometer. H:Q below 0.55 triggers immediate referral to targeted hamstring strengthening.

Q: What happens when a player falls below any baseline norm?
A: The player is referred to Tal Ben-David for an individualised adjustment programme.
