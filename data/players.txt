FC Velocity — Player Profiles and Medical Status

FC Velocity is a professional football club competing in the Israeli Premier League. The medical department consists of lead physiotherapist Dr. Sara Cohen, physiotherapist Dr. Yoel Mizrahi, sports physician Dr. Anat Shapira, and fitness coach Tal Ben-David. All records are maintained in the KineticMind system.

MEDICAL BASELINE NORMS — FC VELOCITY

Resting heart rate target: 42–58 bpm.
Blood pressure normal range: 100/60 – 130/80 mmHg.
Body fat — outfield players: 8–14%. Goalkeepers: 10–16%.
VO2 max minimum — outfield players: 58 ml/kg/min. Goalkeepers: 52 ml/kg/min.
Sit-and-reach flexibility minimum: 28 cm.
Maximum sprint speed minimum — outfield players: 30 km/h.
Hamstring-to-quadriceps strength ratio (H:Q): 0.60–0.70 assessed by isokinetic dynamometer.
Players falling below any baseline are referred to Tal Ben-David for an individualised adjustment programme.
