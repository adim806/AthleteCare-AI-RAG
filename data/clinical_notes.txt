PROTOCOL NOTES FOR DOCUMENTATION

Every clinical note in KineticMind must include: player name and PLR ID, date, treating physiotherapist, session number in the current episode, objective findings (range of motion, pain score, strength measurements), treatment applied, patient response, and plan for the next session. Subjective complaints alone are insufficient — quantitative measurements must be recorded at every session where applicable. Notes must be entered on the day of treatment, not retrospectively.
